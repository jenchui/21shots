A video in a slide

This slide demonstrates how one can have a video in a slide, by simply adding the embed code to the “Video Embed Code” field in the “Kaboodle Custom Settings” panel on the “Slides” Add/Edit form. You can add embed code from any video streaming site that offers an embed code, and it will automatically resize to fit in the slider.

About Kaboodle

Kaboodle is a business theme, designed by Orman Clark, which is super flexible in it's usage. With custom post types for slides, mini-features, portfolio and feedback/testimonials, as well as a theme option's panel you can really make Kaboodle your own.
